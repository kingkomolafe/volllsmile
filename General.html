General Electric (GE)

General Electric (GE) is a multinational conglomerate and one of the largest industrial companies in the United States. While GE has a diverse range of businesses, including power and renewable energy, aviation, healthcare, and more, it has a significant presence in the mechanical appliances sector. GE Appliances, a subsidiary of General Electric, is one of the leading manufacturers of household appliances in the US. They produce a wide range of products, including refrigerators, cooking appliances, dishwashers, washers and dryers, and air conditioners, among others.
